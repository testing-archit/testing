Welcome to Coal Vision

Coal Vision is your go-to platform for all things related to your work with the Ministry of Coal. Here, you can efficiently manage and view your tasks, receive real-time alerts, check your holiday schedule, handle shift handovers, and stay updated with safety measures and mine updates. Our goal is to streamline your workflow and keep you informed with the latest information to ensure a productive and safe work environment.
